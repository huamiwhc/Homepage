About Me

I am an inaugural employee of Huami Co., Ltd., one of the top three wearable data services worldwide. And I am the leader of algorithms development team, and the KEY contributor to sales totaling 22,000,000 Xiaomi Band Series products. My work is mainly on developing algorithms based on IMU sensors (accelerometer, gyroscope and magnetometer) and GPS sensor.

I am Ph.D. candidate student at the Department of Computer Science at University of Science of Technology of China, under the supervision of Dr. Hang Li. My Ph.D. is the Joint-Training Ph.D. with Microsoft Research Asia(MSRA), so I worked in MSRA as a fulltime intern for 4 years. My research direction including Data Mining, Information Retrieval and Natural Language Processing.
